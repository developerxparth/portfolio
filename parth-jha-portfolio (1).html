Who I Am

I'm a first-year BTech student in Mathematics & Computing at South Asian University, New Delhi — a place that pushes me to think rigorously about both the math behind systems and the humans who use them.

My work spans two worlds: I build full-stack web applications (MERN stack, real-time systems, event platforms) and I think deeply about UX/UI design — how software should feel, not just function. I've shipped event ticketing platforms, exam simulators, and auth systems from scratch.

Outside of code and design, I play badminton with an attacking style, watch anime (Death Note, Code Geass, AoT), and spend too much time thinking about probability theory. I believe the best interfaces are invisible, and the best code is the kind that doesn't need comments.
